ANTI-STRAT FORMAT REFERENCE — use this exact style
===========================================================
This is an example of what a professional anti-strat brief looks like.
Copy this format when writing a brief for any team.

-----------------------------------------------------------
HAVEN CT — Key points
-----------------------------------------------------------
DEFAULT: 1-3-1 with Sova dart A main + watch B. Neon setup C long with
Chamber trip. Omen stays mobile short for fast rotations.

PRIORITY: They over-commit A — 3-A aggro is their go-to. C is almost always
weak when they stack A. One anchor on C and fast rotate is enough.

WATCH OUT: Bait setup A long + Omen top B with fast rotator — don't
over-commit either lane before reading util.

WATCH OUT: 3 A aggro with Paranoia from short + Neon running pocket +
Chamber trip top short. Omen stays noob corner. CALL THIS EARLY A MAIN.

WATCH OUT: They fake monkey push window — Chamber peeks deep grass then
Sova drone double door. Don't over-peek the drone.

WATCH OUT: Neon setup Chamber deep C with stun + dart deep. Counter: hold
the angle before they get there, don't let Chamber free-peek.

SOVA ULT: A exec late round with Cypher inserted rabbit B.
OMEN ULT: Fast C with drone, ult green box with god dart + stun 90.

ECO: Force timing — A exec just after 1:23 smoke A long, stun default,
dash right side, flash default. Stay back, don't over-peek early.

Good call: 3 A with Yoru fast long close + Omen short.
Good call: GenG flood when they over-commit C long.
Good call: Pressure B to force them to decide between A and C.

-----------------------------------------------------------
HAVEN T — Key points
-----------------------------------------------------------
WATCH OUT: Fast B exec with entry sliding right to check backsite +
Chamber scaling right. They commit hard once one player gets backsite.

WATCH OUT: A exec at 1:23 smoke + paranoia close + dart close. Early
indication: cam + cage short. Jett smokes long to get out.

WATCH OUT: Very contact team — off-timing 4-man contact A main. Don't
give free space on A long, they will run it.

WATCH OUT: Sova drone early C long = C exec with 1 lurk coming opposite.
If denied C, they end B with a lurk short (usually their sentinel).

WATCH OUT: They like to lurk aggro window during C splits — watch window
every time you hear a C take.

DRONE tells the pack behind it. Fake timing on drone during mid-late round.

If Omen smokes garage early: most likely A exec incoming.

OMEN ULT: Late A short exec after garage pressure.
SOVA ULT: A exec late round with Cypher inserted rabbit B.

Good call: 3 A with Yoru fast long + Omen short — catches them mid-rotate.
Good call: Puppy A so heavy focus B and C.
